# Hi there 👋 I'm Dr. Mohamed El-Sayed M. Essa

### Associate Professor | AI Researcher | Control Systems | Smart Energy | Digital Twins

I am an **Associate Professor of Electrical Engineering** at the Egyptian Aviation Academy with more than **14 years of academic and industrial experience** in Artificial Intelligence, Intelligent Control Systems, Renewable Energy, Digital Twins, Smart Grids, Electric Vehicles, Robotics, and Industrial Automation.

My research bridges **Artificial Intelligence** with **Cyber-Physical Energy Systems** to develop intelligent, sustainable, and autonomous engineering solutions.
